What is MoleculeXR
Research, not simulation.
From day one.

Students investigate a real open scientific question — how HTLV-1 protease inhibitor scaffolds affect polyproline loop dynamics — using the same tools as computational research labs.

Interactive VR Simulations

Students use Narupa iMD-VR to physically interact with molecular dynamics simulations — steering inhibitors, observing hydrogen bonds, watching the polyproline loop flex in real time.

Python Trajectory Analysis

Pre-built Jupyter notebooks guide students through RMSD, RMSF, and H-bond occupancy analysis using MDAnalysis — no coding experience required, just modify and run.

Real Research Question

How do statine, AHPPA, and HEA inhibitor scaffolds differentially restrict HTLV-1 protease polyproline loop flexibility? This is genuinely open — students discover the answer.

Full Instructor Support

15-week implementation guide, VR setup instructions, troubleshooting notes, weekly timing plans, and FAQ — designed for instructors with no prior MD or VR experience.

Equity-First Design

Built for students with no programming or biochemistry background. All computation runs in Google Colab — just a browser needed. Scaffolded for diverse preparation levels.

Assessed & Published

Accompanying manuscript accepted at Journal of College Science Teaching (2026). Includes 6 assessment rubrics emphasizing scientific reasoning over predetermined outcomes.
