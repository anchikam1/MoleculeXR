What is MoleculeXR
Research, not simulation.
From day one.

Students investigate a real open scientific question about HTLV-1 protease inhibitor dynamics using the same tools as computational research labs.

Interactive VR Simulations

Students use Narupa iMD-VR to physically steer inhibitors, observe hydrogen bond formation, and watch the polyproline loop flex in real time.

Python Trajectory Analysis

Pre-built Jupyter notebooks guide RMSD, RMSF, and H-bond occupancy analysis using MDAnalysis — just modify parameters and run.

Genuine Research Question

How do statine, AHPPA, and HEA inhibitors affect HTLV-1 protease loop dynamics? Three teams, three scaffolds, one open question.

Full Instructor Package

15-week guide, VR setup, troubleshooting, timing plans — designed for instructors with no prior MD or VR experience.

Zero Barriers

Runs in Google Colab — just a browser and internet connection. Scaffolded for students with no programming or biochemistry background.

Peer-Reviewed

Manuscript accepted at Journal of College Science Teaching (2026). Six assessment rubrics focused on scientific reasoning process.
