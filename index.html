What is MoleculeXR
VR meets drug discovery — in the classroom

A course-based undergraduate research experience (CURE) where students investigate how HTLV-1 protease inhibitor scaffolds affect polyproline loop dynamics — using iMD-VR, Python, and real simulation data.

Real Research Question

Open scientific problem — how inhibitor scaffolds affect HTLV-1 protease loop dynamics

iMD-VR with Narupa

Students physically interact with molecular dynamics simulations in virtual reality

Python + MDAnalysis

Pre-built Colab notebooks — students modify parameters, interpret results

Zero Prerequisites

Built for General Chemistry II — no biochemistry, no programming experience needed

Python Notebooks
Five notebooks. Zero installation.

Open any notebook directly in Google Colab with one click. Pre-computed fallback data included — works even without trajectory files.

Notebook 01
Colab & Data Analysis
Python fundamentals using calorimetry data. Statistics, error bars, publication-quality plotting. Connects to Gen Chem II thermodynamics.
Weeks 2–3  ·  ~90 min
Open in Colab
Notebook 02
Molecular Data & PDB Files
Parse protein structure files, calculate 3D atomic distances, analyze B-factors as flexibility indicators. Foundation for trajectory work.
Week 4  ·  ~90 min
Open in Colab
Notebook 03
Lysozyme RMSD — Dress Rehearsal
Full trajectory analysis on lysozyme — a known system with published values. RMSD, RMSF, equilibration detection. Built-in quality check.
Weeks 5–6  ·  ~2 hrs
Open in Colab
Notebook 04
Hydrogen Bond Analysis
Implement H-bond geometry from scratch, explore cutoff sensitivity, calculate occupancy, connect to ΔG = −RT ln K thermodynamics.
Weeks 6–7  ·  ~90 min
Open in Colab
Notebook 05 — Research Pipeline
Full HTLV-1 Protease Analysis
The research instrument for Phase 2. Edit only your file name and inhibitor class. Generates RMSD, RMSF, H-bond occupancy, cross-team comparison figures.
Weeks 9–11  ·  ~2 hrs/run  ·  Statine · AHPPA · HEA
Open in Colab
